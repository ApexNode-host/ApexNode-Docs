# Partner Program

ApexNode's Partner Program is a step up from our [Affiliate Program](../affiliate-program/). It's built for content creators, influencers, and gaming communities who want to work more closely with us. Partners get extra perks on top of the standard affiliate commissions.

{: .note}
You must be an **active affiliate** before you can apply for the Partner Program. If you haven't set up your affiliate link yet, check out our [Affiliate Program guide](../affiliate-program/) first.

---

## Who we're looking for

- Content creators and influencers with a passion for gaming
- Streamers, YouTubers, TikTokers, and other creators with engaged audiences
- Gaming communities, Discord servers, or organizations with active members
- People who share values that align with ApexNode's brand

You don't need a massive following to apply, but you should be actively creating content or managing a community.

---

## Partner perks

Once you're approved as a partner, here's what you get:

| Perk | Details |
|:-----|:--------|
| **Free server hosting** | Complimentary game server hosting for supported titles |
| **Exclusive promo codes** | Custom discount codes to share with your community |
| **Profile page** | Your own page on our website showing off you and your brand |
| **Partner Discord channels** | Access to partner-only channels in our Discord |
| **Direct staff contact** | 1-on-1 communication with ApexNode staff |
| **Higher commissions** | Better commission rates through the affiliate program |

---

## How to apply

### Step 1. Become an affiliate

Create an account on our [client dashboard](https://dash.apexnode.host) and activate your affiliate role. Start sharing your referral link and earning commissions.

### Step 2. Build your presence

Share your affiliate link, create content, and stay active in your community. There's no specific number you need to hit, but we want to see that you're putting in the effort.

### Step 3. Request a review

When you're ready, reach out to us on [Discord](https://apexnode.host/discord) and let us know you'd like to be considered for the Partner Program. Our team will review your activity and reach out if it's a good fit.

{: .warning}
Website applications are currently disabled. All partner applications are handled through Discord.

---

## Questions?

If you have any questions about the Partner Program or want to know if you'd be a good fit, reach out to us on [Discord](https://apexnode.host/discord) or submit a [support ticket](https://dash.apexnode.host/tickets/create).
