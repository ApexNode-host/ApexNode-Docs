# Affiliate Program

Earn commissions by referring people to ApexNode. It's free, easy to set up, and great for content creators, community leaders, or anyone who wants to share ApexNode with others.

---

## How it works

1. Create an account on our [client dashboard](https://dash.apexnode.host)
2. Set up your unique affiliate code in your [dashboard settings](https://dash.apexnode.host)
3. Copy your unique affiliate link and share it with others
4. Earn commissions on all sales made through your link, including recurring monthly subscriptions

Once someone signs up through your link and makes a purchase, you earn a commission. Simple as that.

---

## Commission rates

Commissions start at **10%** and increase based on your total sales volume, up to a maximum of **25%**.

| Sales volume | Commission rate |
|:-------------|:----------------|
| Starting rate | 10% |
| Maximum rate | 25% |

Commission payouts are processed **3 days** after each sale. You earn on every payment your referrals make, including recurring monthly subscriptions - not just the first one.

---

## Who is it for?

The affiliate program is open to everyone, but it's especially useful for:

- **Content creators** - YouTubers, streamers, TikTokers
- **Community leaders** - Discord server owners, forum admins
- **Server owners** - recommend ApexNode to friends and other communities
- **Anyone** who wants to earn by sharing something they use

---

## Getting started

### Step 1. Create an account

If you don't have one already, create an account on our [client dashboard](https://dash.apexnode.host).

### Step 2. Set up your affiliate code

Log in to your dashboard and look for the affiliate settings. From there you can create a custom affiliate code and grab your unique referral link.

### Step 3. Share your link

Share your affiliate link wherever you'd like - your Discord server, YouTube description, stream panels, social media, etc. When someone clicks your link and makes a purchase, the commission gets tracked automatically.

### Step 4. Get paid

Commissions are processed 3 days after each sale. You can keep track of your referrals and earnings right from your dashboard.
